Instant Ramen

You know what this is too...

Ingredients

  • Ramen packet
  • 550ml Water
  • Pot
  • Stove

Steps

  1. Put 550 ml of water in pot
  2. Put pot on stove and bring to a boil
  3. When boiling, put everything in ramen packet into boiling water
  4. Wait 4 minutes
  5. EAT
